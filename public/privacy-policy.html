Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

Parkvana ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for Parkvana, we collect:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

Location Information

To provide our parking marketplace services, we collect:

  • Your current location when searching for parking spots
  • The address of parking spaces you list
  • GPS coordinates for mapping and navigation features

Transaction Information

We collect information about your parking transactions, including:

  • Booking details (date, time, duration, location)
  • Payment history
  • Earnings from listed parking spaces

Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • App usage data and analytics
  • Log data (IP address, access times, pages viewed)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our parking marketplace services
  • Process your bookings and payments
  • Show you nearby parking spots based on your location
  • Enable communication between parking space owners and renters
  • Send you booking confirmations and receipts
  • Track and fulfill our veteran support mission ($1 per booking donation)
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Send you important updates about our service
  • Respond to your support requests

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in these limited circumstances:

With Other Users

  • When you book a parking spot, the owner sees your name and booking details
  • When someone books your parking spot, you see their name and booking details
  • Location of listed parking spaces is visible to all users searching for parking

With Service Providers

  • Payment processors (for secure payment handling)
  • Cloud hosting services (for data storage)
  • Analytics providers (to improve our service)
  • Email service providers (for transactional emails)

With Veteran Support Organizations

  • We share aggregated donation data (no personal information) with our veteran support partners

For Legal Reasons

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and property
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activity
  • To protect the safety of our users

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Location Data

Parkvana requires access to your location to:

  • Show you nearby parking spots on the map
  • Calculate distances from your current location
  • Provide navigation to your booked parking spot
  • Verify the location of parking spaces you list

You can control location permissions through your device settings. Disabling location services will limit app functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure password storage (hashed and salted)
  • Regular security audits
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Secure payment processing (PCI DSS compliant processors)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You can request account deletion at any time by contacting us.

After account deletion:

  • Your personal information will be deleted within 30 days
  • Transaction records may be retained for legal and accounting purposes
  • Anonymized data may be retained for analytics

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct your information in the app
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (transactional emails will still be sent)
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@parkvanahq.com

8. Children's Privacy

Parkvana is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

We do not sell your personal information.

10. International Users

Parkvana is based in the United States. If you access our service from outside the US, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

11. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. Please review their privacy policies.

12. Push Notifications

We may send you push notifications about:

  • Booking confirmations
  • Upcoming reservations
  • Earnings notifications
  • Important service updates

You can disable push notifications in your device settings.

13. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app usage
  • Improve our services

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of Parkvana after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@parkvanahq.com
Support: support@parkvanahq.com
Website: parkvanahq.com
